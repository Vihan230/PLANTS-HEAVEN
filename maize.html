MAZE

Tall green plants crowned with golden cobs.

Growing Requirements
            🌍 Climate: Warm, subtropical
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 18°C – 35°C
            📍 Location: Full sunlight, fertile well-drained soil
            💧 Water Need:
            New plant: Water 2–3 times/week
            After established: Once/week
            Avoid stagnant water ❌
