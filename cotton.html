COTTON

“Cotton — Soft by Nature, Strong by Design.” 🌿🤍

Growing Requirements
            🌍 Climate: Warm, dry tropical
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 21°C – 35°C
            📍 Location: Full sunlight, black cotton soil or well-drained soil
            💧 Water Need:
            New plant: Water once/week
            After established: Moderate irrigation
            Excess water affects fiber quality ❌
