“Garlic — Bold Cloves, Big Flavor.” 🧄🌿"

Growing Requirements
            🌍 Climate: Cool, dry
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 10°C – 25°C
            📍 Location: Full sunlight, loose well-drained soil
            💧 Water Need:
            New plant: Water once/week
            After established: Light irrigation only
            Avoid excess water ❌
