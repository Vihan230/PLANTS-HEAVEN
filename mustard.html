MUSTARD

“Mustard — Bold Seeds, Bright Flavor.” 🌼🌿

Growing Requirements
            🌍 Climate: Cool to moderate
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 10°C – 25°C
            📍 Location: Full sunlight, fertile well-drained soil
            💧 Water Need:
            New plant: Water once/week
            After established: Irrigate at flowering & pod stage
            Avoid excess moisture ❌
