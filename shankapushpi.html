SHANKAPUSHPI

**“Shankhpushpi in a pot—nature’s calm for a sharper mind.”** 🌿

Growing Requirement
            🌍 Climate: Tropical & Subtropical, war
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 20°C – 35°
            📍 Location: Full sunlight, open area, sandy-loam well-drained soi
            💧 Water Need:
            New plant: Water 2–3 times/week
            After established: Water once/week
            Avoid waterlogging ✅
