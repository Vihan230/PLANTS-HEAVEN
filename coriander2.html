CORIANDER

“Coriander — Freshness That Finishes Every Dish.” 🌿✨

Growing Requirements
            🌍 Climate: Cool, sunny
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 15°C – 25°C
            📍 Location: Full sunlight, loose well-drained soil
            💧 Water Need: 
            New plant: Water 2 times/week
            After established: Once/week
            Avoid waterlogging ❌
