GROUNDNUT

“Groundnut — Earthy Goodness in Every Bite.” 🌱🥜

Growing Requirements
            🌍 Climate: Warm tropical to subtropical
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 20°C – 35°C
            📍 Location: Full sunlight, sandy-loam well-drained soil
            💧 Water Need:
            New plant: Water 2 times/week
            After established: Once/week
            Avoid waterlogging (pods rot) ❌
