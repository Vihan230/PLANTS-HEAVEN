CARDMOM

Cardamom – the Queen of Spices that turns every dish into pure aromatic magic. 🌿✨

1. **Temperature:** Ideal 20–30°C (tolerates 15–35°C); cannot withstand frost or extreme heat.
            **Rainfall & Humidity:** Needs 1500–4000 mm annual rainfall and high humidity; avoid waterlogging.
            **Light:** Grows best under 50–60% shade; direct harsh sunlight damages plants.
            **Soil:** Well-drained, rich loamy soil with high organic matter and slightly acidic pH (5.5–6.5).
            **Care Essentials:** Regular irrigation in dry periods, mulching, organic manure, and pest/disease control.
