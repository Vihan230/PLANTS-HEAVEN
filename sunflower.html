SUNFLOWER

“Sunflower — Turning Toward the Light.” 🌻✨

Growing Requirements
            🌍 Climate: Warm, sunny
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 20°C – 35°C
            📍 Location: Full sunlight, deep well-drained soil
            💧 Water Need:
            New plant: Water 2 times/week
            After established: Once/week
            Avoid stagnant water ❌
