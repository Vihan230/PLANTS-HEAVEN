SOYBEAN

“Soybean — Small Bean, Big Nutrition.” 🌱✨

Growing Requirements
            🌍 Climate: Warm & humid subtropical
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 20°C – 30°C
            📍 Location: Full sunlight, well-drained loamy soil
            💧 Water Need:
            New plant: Water 2 times/week
            After established: Once/week
            Avoid waterlogging especially during flowering ❌
