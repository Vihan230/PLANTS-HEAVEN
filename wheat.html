Timeless golden stalks feeding generations.

Growing Requirements
            🌍 Climate: Temperate to subtropical
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 10°C – 25°C
            📍 Location: Full sunlight, open fields, well-drained loamy soil
            💧 Water Need:
            New plant: Water every 7–10 days
            After established: Irrigate at critical stages (tillering, flowering)
            Avoid waterlogging ❌
