ARJUNA TREE

**“Arjuna tree in a pot—strong roots for a healthy heart.”** 🌳

Growing Requirement
            🌍 Climate: Subtropical to tropica
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 20°C – 38°
            📍 Location: Full sunlight, deep fertile soil, near open ground (often near water bodies
            💧 Water Need:
            New plant: Water 2 times/week
            After established: Water once/week
            Avoid prolonged waterlogging ❌
