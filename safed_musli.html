SAFED MUSLI

**“Safed Musli in a pot—pure strength, grown naturally.”** 🌱

Growing Requirement
            🌍 Climate: Warm, dry to semi-humi
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 20°C – 32°
            📍 Location: Partial to full sunlight, loose sandy soil, good drainag
            💧 Water Need:
            New plant: Water 2 times/week
            After established: Water once/week
            Excess water causes root rot ❌
