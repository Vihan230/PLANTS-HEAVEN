SATAVARI

**“Shatavari in a pot—gentle nourishment, natural balance.”** 🌱

Growing Requirements
            🌍 Climate: Tropical to subtropical, war
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 22°C – 35°C
            📍 Location
            Partial to full sunlight, open airy place, well-drained sandy-loam soil, support needed for climbing
            💧 Water Need:
            New plant: Water 2–3 times/week
            After established: Water once/week (reduce during rainy season)
            Avoid overwatering / waterlogging ✅
