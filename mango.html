MANGO

Young mango plant – the first step toward a fruitful future.

Growing Requirements
                🌍 Climate: Tropical / Subtropical, warm climate
                🌡️ Temperature: Best 24°C – 35°📍
                Location: Full sunlight (6–8+ hours/day), open airy place, well-drained soil
                💧 Water Need:
                New plant: water 2–3 times/week
                After established: water once/week (less in rainy season)
                Avoid overwatering / waterlogging ✅
