PIGEON PEA

“Pigeon Pea — Strong Crop, Steady Nourishment.” 🌱🌾

Growing Requirements
            🌍 Climate: Warm, semi-arid
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 20°C – 35°C
            📍 Location: Full sunlight, deep well-drained soil
            💧 Water Need:
            New plant: Water once/week
            After established: Rare irrigation
            Highly drought tolerant ✅
