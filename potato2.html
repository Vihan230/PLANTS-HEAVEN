POTATO

“Potato — Humble Root, Endless Possibilities.” 🥔🌿

Growing Requirements
            🌍 Climate: Cool temperate
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 15°C – 25°C
            📍 Location: Full sunlight, loose sandy-loam soil
            💧 Water Need:
            New plant: Water 2 times/week
            After established: Once/week
            Avoid excess water near harvest ❌
