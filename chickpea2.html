CHICKPEA

“Chickpea — Tiny Pulse, Mighty Power.” 🌱✨ 

Growing Requirements
            🌍 Climate: Cool, semi-arid
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 10°C – 25°C
            📍 Location: Full sunlight, light well-drained soil
            💧 Water Need:
            New plant: Light watering once/week
            After established: Very limited irrigation
            Excess water reduces yield ❌
