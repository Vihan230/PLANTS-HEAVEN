Growing Requirements
            🌍 Climate: Tropical & subtropical, warm and humid
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 20°C – 35°C
            📍 Location: Full sunlight, lowland or irrigated fields, clayey to loamy soil
            💧 Water Need:
            New plant: Maintain shallow flooding / irrigate frequently
            After established: Standing water or regular irrigation
            Waterlogging required during growth stage ✅
