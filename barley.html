Growing Requirements
            🌍 Climate: Cool temperate
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 12°C – 25°C
            📍 Location: Full sunlight, dry well-drained soil
            💧 Water Need:
            New plant: Light watering once/week
            After established: Minimal irrigation
            Excess water reduces yield ❌
