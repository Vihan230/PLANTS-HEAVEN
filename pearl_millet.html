PEARL MILLET

“Pearl Millet — Tiny Grains, Mighty Strength.” 🌾✨ 

Growing Requirements
            🌍 Climate: Arid & semi-arid
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 25°C – 40°C
            📍 Location: Full sunlight, dry sandy soil
            💧 Water Need
            New plant: Water once/week
            After established: Minimal irrigation
            Drought tolerant crop ✅
