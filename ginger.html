GINGER

**“Fresh ginger in a pot—homegrown spice with healing vibes.”** 🌱

Growing Requirement
            🌍 Climate: Warm, humi
            🌡️ Temperature: 20°C – 35°
            📍 Location: Partial shade, loose soi
            💧 Water Need:
            New plant: 2–3 times/week
            After established: Regular moisture
            Avoid waterlogging ❌
