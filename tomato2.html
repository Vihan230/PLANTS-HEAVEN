TOMATO

“Tomato — Sun-Kissed Flavor in Every Bite.” 🍅🌿

Growing Requirements
            🌍 Climate: Warm, sunny
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 18°C – 32°C
            📍 Location: Full sunlight, support needed, well-drained soil
            💧 Water Need:
            New plant: Water 2–3 times/week
            After established: Regular watering
            Avoid waterlogging ❌
