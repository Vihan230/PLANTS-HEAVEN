LENTIL

“Lentil — Small Seed, Big Nourishment.” 🌱✨

Growing Requirements
            🌍 Climate: Cool temperate
            🌡️ Temperature: Best 10°C – 25°C
            📍 Location: Full sunlight, light loamy soil
            💧 Water Need:
            New plant: Water once/week
            After established: Occasional irrigation
            Avoid waterlogging ❌
